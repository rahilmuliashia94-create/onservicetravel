About On Service Travel Consultants (OSTC)

On Service Travel Consultants (OSTC) is a Government of India–authorized overseas manpower recruitment and workforce deployment firm headquartered in Rajkot, Gujarat. We specialize in placing skilled, semi-skilled, and professional Indian workers into verified international job markets across Europe, the Middle East, Israel, and North America.

Who We Are

OSTC is not a typical visa or travel agent. We operate as a full-cycle international recruitment partner, managing everything from candidate screening and trade testing to visa processing, legal compliance, and post-deployment support.

Our core objective is simple and uncompromising: to create legal, ethical, and sustainable overseas employment opportunities for Indian workers while delivering reliable manpower solutions to global employers.

What Makes OSTC Different

1. Government Authorization & Legal Compliance

OSTC operates strictly under Indian Emigration Act regulations and adheres to destination-country labor laws.

Every deployment is contract-verified, salary-transparent, embassy-compliant, and free from illegal brokerage practices.

We do not process fake job offers, tourist-to-work conversions, or unauthorized middlemen transactions.

2. End-to-End Recruitment Model

Most agencies stop at visa stamping. OSTC goes further.

Our services include candidate sourcing & verification, trade testing, language orientation, medical & police clearance coordination, visa documentation, employer liaison, flight booking, and post-arrival coordination.

3. In-House Training & Trade Testing – Rajkot

Our Rajkot Training Center ensures candidates are job-ready before departure.

Training includes trade-specific skill validation, safety standards, worksite behavior, basic English or country-specific language exposure, and cultural expectations education.

This reduces rejection risk and improves employer satisfaction.

4. Zero-Exploitation Policy

OSTC follows a strict Ethical Recruitment Charter.

No illegal fees. No document confiscation. No false salary promises. No contract substitution.

Every candidate receives a written job contract, full salary explanation, and legal exit clearance guidance.
