Phase 01: Service Canada (ESDC) Compliance

The Labour Market Test

01

Advertising & Recruitment Audit ST-01-B

We manage the mandatory 30-day "Market Test" required by ESDC. In 2026, Service Canada has shifted from simple ad-checking to forensic recruitment audits. Our process ensures you meet the highest standard of "Active Candidate Engagement."

  • Job Bank Match Requirement: We manage your Job Bank profile to ensure all "4-star" and "5-star" Canadian candidates are manually invited within the first 14 days. Failure to invite qualified domestic leads is the #1 cause of LMIA refusal in 2026.
  • Multi-Platform Saturation: Strategic placement on 2 additional national platforms. For NOC 73300 (Transport), we utilize trucking-specific boards; for NOC 85100 (Agriculture), we target regional agricultural bulletins to prove local scarcity.
  • Forensic Recruitment Summary: We compile the Recruitment Summary Report. This documents every resume received, the date of interview, and the specific, non-discriminatory technical reasons for non-selection (e.g., lack of specific provincial licensure or inability to meet safety-sensitive heavy lifting requirements).
  • Record Retention: We organize your "Audit Binder" containing proof of payment for all ads and digital copies of all applications, which must be stored for 6 years as per IRPR regulations.
02

NOC 2021 & Wage Analysis TEER 0-5

We perform a technical audit of your job offer. Under-offering by even $0.25 below the regional median will trigger an immediate system rejection.

  • NOC 2021 Alignment: Precise mapping of duties to TEER categories. We ensure your job description for a "Heavy-Duty Mechanic" doesn't accidentally trigger a "Service Technician" classification, which carries different wage requirements.
  • Wage Integrity Audit: We verify your offer against the Economic Region Median. If your region (e.g., Lethbridge vs. Calgary) has a higher prevailing wage for your NOC, we adjust the offer to ensure it is "Audit-Proof" against CRA cross-referencing.
  • Transition Plan Drafting: For High-Wage positions, we draft the mandatory Transition Plan, outlining how you will reduce reliance on foreign workers through future domestic training or Permanent Residency support for your current staff.
03

Workforce Cap & Regional Audit 6% RULE

New 2026 directives strictly limit Low-Wage hiring based on regional economic health. We navigate these barriers to keep your pipeline open.

  • The 6% Refusal Rule: As of early 2026, Low-Wage LMIAs are refused in regions with 6% or higher unemployment. We conduct a pre-filing audit of your Census Metropolitan Area (CMA) and pivot to High-Wage or Agriculture streams if your region is restricted.
  • TFW Cap Calculation: We calculate your current 10% or 20% (sector-specific) workforce cap. We ensure your "total head count" includes all active TFWs and pending applications to prevent over-quota rejections.
  • Primary Agriculture Exemptions: We leverage exemptions for the Seasonal Agricultural Worker Program (SAWP) and the Agricultural Stream, ensuring farm owners are not subject to the 6% regional refusal rules.

Phase 02: Source-Country Integrity (India)

Zero-Fraud Verification Pipeline

PSK-PCC & Federal Security

Liability for fraud falls on the Canadian employer. To protect you, OSTC sources only via Passport Seva Kendra (PSK). We verify federal Police Clearance Certificates via digital QR-code tracking. This ensures zero criminal history and prevents Section 40 Misrepresentation charges that could lead to your company being banned from the TFWP for 5 years.

Practical Trade Testing (Rajkot)

Resumes are not reality. At our Rajkot Center, we conduct physical trade tests. For fleet owners, our drivers must demonstrate Pre-Trip Inspection proficiency and air-brake knowledge. For farm owners, labourers are tested on equipment safety and chemical handling. You only interview candidates who have already passed our technical gatekeepers.

IRCC Medical & Bio-Readiness

We coordinate "Upfront Medicals" with IRCC-authorized Panel Physicians in India. By screening for communicable diseases and health issues before the LMIA is even issued, we eliminate the 3-month medical delays that often happen at the visa stage. We ensure every worker has their biometrics booked at VFS Global within 48 hours of work permit submission.

Phase 03: IRCC & VFS Global

Deployment & Visa Stamping

04

Work Permit Submission IMM 1295

Once the Positive LMIA is received, the file moves to the Visa Office Abroad. OSTC manages the 100% digital submission to the IRCC portal.

  • Employer Portal Compliance: We manage the submission of the Offer of Employment through the GCKey portal and verify the payment of the $230 Employer Compliance Fee.
  • Family & Schedule 1 Filings: We handle the complex IMM 5257 (Schedule 1) forms, ensuring a worker's past military service or travel history is disclosed accurately to prevent Security Inadmissibility delays.
  • Port of Entry (POE) Briefing: We prepare the worker for the CBSA Interview. They are trained to present their Letter of Introduction and explain their job duties to the border officer, ensuring their Work Permit is issued correctly upon arrival at YYZ or YVR.
05

Housing & Post-Arrival Audit

Your compliance obligations continue after the worker arrives. OSTC provides the framework for long-term audit defense.

  • Housing Standards: For Agricultural and Low-Wage streams, we verify that your worker housing meets CMHC standards (Suitability & Affordability). We assist in coordinating the mandatory 8-month housing inspection reports required for LMIA renewals.
  • CRA Payroll Alignment: We ensure your payroll deductions (CPP, EI) match the Employment Agreement. During a random Service Canada inspection, we provide the documentation to prove the worker is being paid the exact wage promised in the LMIA.
  • Worker Rights Orientation: To satisfy IRPR Section 209.2, we provide the worker with a manual on their rights in Canada, protecting you from "abuse" or "mistreatment" allegations during future government audits.
