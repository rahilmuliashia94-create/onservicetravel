Phase 01: Service Canada (ESDC) Compliance

The Labour Market Test

01

Advertising & Recruitment Audit ST-01-B

  • 14-day minimum Job Bank activity with 4-star+ Job Match invites.
  • Placement on 2 additional national platforms.
  • Recruitment Summary Report drafting.
02

NOC 2021 & Wage Analysis TEER 0-5

  • Alignment with NOC 73300 or NOC 85100.
  • Wage verification against Economic Region Median.
  • Transition Plan drafting for High-Wage Stream.
03

Workforce Cap & Regional Audit 2026 UPDATE

  • TFW Cap calculations (10–20%).
  • Unemployment rate (6% rule) monitoring.
04

LMIA File Drafting & Risk Shield EMP5626

  • Completion of EMP5626 / EMP5627 forms.
  • CRA document verification & payroll audit.
  • Provincial Employment Standards alignment.
  • Preparation for ESDC Employer Compliance Inspection.
  • Housing compliance review (Agricultural Stream).
