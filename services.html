Visa & Work Permit Categories We Support

Short-Term Work Visa

Suitable for contract-based employment up to 6–12 months. Common in seasonal industries, agriculture, and temporary construction projects.

Long-Term Work Visa

Multi-year employment contracts for construction, manufacturing, logistics, healthcare, and skilled trades.

Seasonal Work Visa

Agricultural, hospitality, and peak-season employment programs in Europe and North America.

Residence Permit / Residency Visa

Issued after arrival in many EU countries. Allows long-term legal stay under employment contract.

EU Blue Card

For qualified professionals meeting salary and skill criteria. Enables long-term residence and mobility within the European Union.

Employer-Sponsored Work Permit

Country-specific permits approved by the employer prior to embassy visa stamping.
