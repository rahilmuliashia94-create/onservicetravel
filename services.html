Our Services

Overseas Job Placement Services

Our overseas job placement service is designed to help candidates secure stable and legitimate employment opportunities abroad. We work closely with verified international employers and recruitment partners to match candidates based on skill, experience, and job suitability.

Our placement support includes:

  • Candidate screening and profile evaluation
  • Employer requirement analysis
  • Interview scheduling and preparation
  • Offer letter verification
  • Travel coordination and guidance
  • Post-departure follow-ups

We ensure that every candidate understands their job role, salary structure, working conditions, and employer expectations before departure.

International Recruitment Consultancy

For employers, we provide structured recruitment consultancy services that streamline the hiring process and reduce operational burden. Our recruitment model focuses on quality over quantity, ensuring long-term workforce retention.

Our recruitment consultancy includes:

  • Workforce requirement analysis
  • Talent sourcing from India
  • Skill-based screening and shortlisting
  • Interview coordination
  • Compliance-oriented documentation support
  • Deployment planning

Manpower Solutions & Staffing

We offer manpower solutions for organizations requiring skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled workforce support. Our staffing solutions are flexible and scalable, designed to meet short-term, project-based, or long-term manpower needs.

Industries we support include:

  • Hospitality and hotel services
  • Facility management and cleaning services
  • Office and administrative support
  • Commercial and general labor
  • Maintenance and service roles

Training & Skill Development

Our training programs prepare candidates for international work environments by improving job-specific technical skills, workplace discipline, communication, and cultural adaptability.

Training ensures candidates are employer-ready and capable of maintaining long-term employment abroad.
